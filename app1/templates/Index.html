How Are Phishing Websites Used?

First, the user clicks on the "New Register" button and fills in their email and password. After registration, they can log in using the same email and password by clicking the "Login" button.

Once logged in, the user is taken to their personal home dashboard, where their email is displayed along with a "Logout" button and a "Generate Instagram Phishing Link" button.

When the user clicks the phishing link generator, a unique link is created. They can share this link with their friends by saying, “This website helps you increase your Instagram followers.”

When someone clicks and logs in through the phishing page, their Instagram username and password are saved. This data is then visible on the user’s home dashboard.

The user can now use these credentials to log into the Instagram account of the victim.
